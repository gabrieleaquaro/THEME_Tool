EVALUATION OF DIRECT BARRIERS

Based on the observations of the work environment conducted at the site, select the answer that best reflects the observed behaviors:

Safe performance of tasks

In the workplace, does the worker:
performs tasks in a safe manner?

follow the safety procedures even in situations of high time pressure?

take care of the quality of performance in terms of safety?

follow the safety procedures even when the tasks are easy?

pay attention to safety even when there is a lot of work to be done?

Total: 0.5

Compliance with safety rules/procedures

In the workplace, does the worker:
comply with safety standards even when they are not easy to apply?

apply the correct safety procedures to perform the tasks in a safe manner?

use all Personal Protective Equipment required by law?

ensure full compliance with safety rules/standards in the performance of tasks?

actively work to enforce the safety rules for external companies?

Total: 0.5

Safe performance in the context

In the workplace, does the worker:
work to ensure that the work environment is safe?

also act for the safety of colleagues?

monitor the work environment in terms of safety?

take care of the context from a safety perspective even when there is time pressure?

help colleagues make the work environment safer?

Total: 0.5

Active participation in safety

In the workplace, the worker:
undertake to report near misses?

report the risks and hazards when necessary?

spontaneously offer suggestions for improving safety?

where necessary, call for improvements in workplace safety?

when the opportunity arises, report damage to structures and equipment?

Total: 0.5

Teamwork

Within the working groups, do workers, with respect to safety:
support other colleagues in carrying out their work in general?

help to resolve conflicts in the working group?

help others in the performance of tasks?

support colleagues even in situations of high time pressure?

also support colleagues they do not normally work with?

Total: 0.5

Safety communication

Within the working groups, the workers, with respect to safety issues / problems:
give information/feedback about their work?

ask for information/feedback on their work?

clearly and effectively express their opinions and emotions?

have easy access to safety information?

count on the availability of supervisors in sharing information?

feel they are listened to by the supervisors with respect to suggestions for improvement?

Total: 0.5
